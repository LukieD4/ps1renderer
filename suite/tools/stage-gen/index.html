Viewport

Drag a model from the Assets tab into the viewport to place it under the cursor (it rests on whatever surface is there), or double-click the model to drop it at the center of the view. Drag the axis gizmo (top-right of the viewport) to snap the view, like Blender's. The Editor/PS1 toggle next to it switches between unlit editing and a preview of the runtime's GTE lighting from the stage's authored Lights. Number fields are drag-to-scrub: drag vertically to nudge, click to type.
