bit / depth . tools

Small offline tools for pushing pixels around. Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

BPP Image Tool
Reduce an image to a restricted color depth (4/8bpp), adjust hue/saturation/contrast, dither, edit the palette by hand, and export or save your project.
Palette Maker
Create and edit custom color palettes for your projects.
Scene Generator
Generate random pixel art scenes with a variety of settings and export them as PNGs.
