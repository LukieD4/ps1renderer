ps1renderer / Suite of Tools

Small offline tools for pushing pixels around. Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Stage Maker

A standalone tool, should cover the essentials for curating levels.

StageGen
Generate stages with a variety of systems such as trigger volumes, post-processing, SFX and VFX.
Requires the local server (node serve.js) to be running; not from file:// -- Run start.bat to fix this

Texture Curation

Perfect for adding textures to your universe with this tool that's built for the whole TIM pipeline.

TimGen
Supports rescale, CLUTs, reduced bpp, rescale, adjust hue/sat/contrast, dither.

Sound Manipulation

Tools for working with PS1-style audio, including conversion and playback.

1Audio Converter to VAG
Convert a .wav into a PS1 SPU-ADPCM .VAG, with sample-rate, effort and loop controls, and A/B preview.
2OST Studio
Play VAG/WAV instruments through an in-browser SPU engine — polyphonic, with ADSR, pitch, the 24-voice limit, and MIDI-keyboard input. The music-making side, built on the same ADPCM core.
