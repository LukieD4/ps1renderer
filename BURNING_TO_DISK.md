### Booting



I have on record got the ~~bastard~~ to load twice, once at 17:43, then 21:55 (4 hours)



+Good morning, 09:50 here. **I found the technique.**





Tonyhax (100% works but sluggish on loading)



Works on a x24 disk. (bad load times,  perhaps its variable)

Works on a x32 disk. (good load times, perhaps its variable)



1. Boot, prop PS1 horizontal, lid open, disk in
2. As mem card animation flash finishes, close lid
3. Tonyhax loads, prop PS1 vertical
4. Wobble the PS1 side to side from the top (a bit more than gentle, less going rough)



I think depending on the disk speed, you have to be more rough, at least I did with 32x because Tonyhax produces a "type D code 22" error meaning the disk didn't get read.



**I'll always prefer Tonyhax now** since Unirom makes ONE attempt and if it fucks it then it's a reboot.



\*note, if stuck on the last message "Starting" and nothing happens, your console has crashed.
